Brownies Recipe

Brownies are fudgy, chocolatey bars that are rich and chewy. They're often made with butter, sugar, and cocoa.

They're perfect warm or at room temperature, often enjoyed with ice cream.

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: Melt butter and combine with sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Stir in flour, cocoa powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 25-30 minutes until set.
