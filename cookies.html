Cookies Recipe

Cookies are small, sweet treats made from dough with ingredients like butter and sugar. They can be soft, chewy, or crunchy.

Great for snacking or sharing, cookies are a classic favorite.

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together until smooth, then mix in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Add dry ingredients: Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Scoop and bake: Drop spoonfuls of dough on the sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.
